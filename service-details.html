Our Process

At Seal Waterproofing, we use a streamlined four-step method that ensures each project is delivered on time, within budget, and fully compliant with safety and quality standards.

01
Comprehensive Inspection

On-site moisture mapping, crack diagnosis, and structural evaluation to pinpoint all failure points and hidden voids.

02
Engineered Proposal

Customized system selection (membrane, coating, injection, or tanking) plus a step-by-step project plan and warranty package.

03
Expert Installation

Certified field teams execute preparation, repair, application and curing in strict sequence for maximum durability.

04
Final Validation & Care

Leak testing, quality certification, handover report, plus optional annual maintenance plans to protect your investment.
