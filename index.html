How long does waterproofing last?

Our systems include a 10-year product warranty and aftercare plan. With regular inspections, the seal can last 15+ years in most climates.

Do I need to prepare the site before work begins?

We perform entire pre-construction prep: cleaning, crack stitching, and substrate repairs. Your only task is to clear furniture and valuables from the area to be sealed.

Can you waterproof concrete basements with active leaks?

Totally. We use a combination of injection grouting and external tanking to stop active seepage, followed by an internal waterproof membrane for redundant protection.

How long does the cure time take?

Typical cure is 24-48 hours for fast-dry polyurethane top coats; 5-7 days for cementitious and crystalline systems before full service exposure. We provide exact timelines in your project quote.

What payment options are available?

We accept bank transfers, UPI/QR, and major cards. A 30% booking advance secures materials and scheduling, with the remainder after completion and handover.

Need a leak inspection right away?

Our specialists can visit within 24 hours, evaluate your structure, and recommend a full waterproofing strategy tailored to your needs. We cover roof, balcony, bathroom, basement and facade systems, and we provide transparent pricing with photos, moisture readings, and remediation milestones so you know exactly what gets done and when. Don’t wait until the next rainstorm causes damage — immediate action preserves property value.
